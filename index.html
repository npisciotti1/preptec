Introduction:

I've setup this simple page to showcase some of my recent work. Truthfully, I've been neglecting my portfolio as of late! I'll do some light background.

I currently work for a Software Company out of Olympia called "Aesthetics Pro Online." We build a comprehensive suite of POS, management and marketing tools for medical-based practices. Our clients include: Sonobello, Albany Laser Spa, ADSCI (dermatology clinic) and more!

What's really exciting is that every internal tool, service and feature has been developed in-house. This includes our physical server bank and datacenters - we build everything we use!

My role is overseeing our E-Records suite. We provide a HIPAA compliant medical records system that integrates with every process in our software. Considering the number of tools we offer within our platform, my work spans: design, logic integration, code maintenance and testing.

I also do a large amount of internal design for our client-facing software and for our own website. Most of this work I'm unable to share, but I've included some work samples from the past.
